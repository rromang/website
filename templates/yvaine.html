Her Majesty:

Yvaine

My family's loving companion for the past year.

Yvaine's short bio:   

Yvaine (pronounced: E-vain) is a British short hair cat, blue variety.
She gets her name from the book, later made into movie "Stardust" (which I must confess have not read yet but definitely seen the movie).
She was born April 2021 and has been part of our family since July 2021.

She was a tiny thing...

Still unsure about her place in the family.

But, like most cats...

Boxes are a favorite from the beginning.

She started getting comfortable...

But was alert of her new environment.

And, she is a master of disguise...

Mostly because, for some reason, I keep buying gray blankets.

Now, clearly at ease...

She can be "flexible" in how and where she relaxes.

But most importantly...

She is a regal cat, which why she gets to be called every once in a while "Her Majesty".
